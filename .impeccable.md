## Design Context

### Users

AIDI serves employees inside Yadea's enterprise OA environment, including general office staff, research and business analysts, content producers, agent creators, and platform administrators. They use it repeatedly during the workday to retrieve enterprise knowledge, analyze files, run multi-step agent tasks, and turn results into deliverables.

### Brand Personality

Clear, trustworthy, and capable. The product should feel like a restrained enterprise workbench: calm enough for sustained use, explicit about system state, and confident without looking promotional or experimental.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use the supplied AIDI 2.0 Figma and frontend specification as the source of truth. The employee workspace uses a light blue-violet page background, white working surfaces, the AIDI purple brand accent, restrained gradients, light shadows, compact information hierarchy, and a PC-first enterprise layout. The platform management area remains visually distinct from the employee workspace.

### Design Principles

1. Organize the interface around work tasks rather than model brands.
2. Keep navigation stable and preserve the five-module employee architecture.
3. Prioritize task input, execution state, results, and continuation over decoration.
4. Use the shared spacing, type, color, radius, shadow, motion, and z-index tokens consistently.
5. Make every major workflow keyboard-operable and complete across loading, empty, error, disabled, and success states.
